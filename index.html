FAQs

Is Puplar a bank?

No, Puplar is not a bank. It is a financial infrastructure platform that connects businesses to regulated banking, payment, and settlement rails while providing a unified system to manage those workflows.

Can I collect payments without writing code?

Yes, Puplar offers no-code solutions such as payment links and easy-to-use dashboards to help you collect payments without technical integration.

Does Puplar support bulk payouts?

Absolutely. Our platform is designed to handle high-volume disbursements and bulk payouts across multiple currencies and regions efficiently.

Can I embed payments directly into my product?

Yes, our robust APIs allow you to embed seamless payment experiences directly into your own applications and platforms.

How can I integrate with Puplar?

You can integrate via our developer-friendly documentation, which provides SDKs and API references for various programming languages.
